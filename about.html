About Oqkitob

Oqkitob is a workspace platform built to help people keep notes, tasks, and structured information in one organized place.

Our goal is to create a simple system where each user can open purpose-based books for different areas of life and work, from quick notes to tasks and finance tracking.

We are starting with a focused web experience that combines a modern app workflow with lightweight public pages that are easy to discover and read.
